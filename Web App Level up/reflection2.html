Reflection #2



The truth is, I enjoyed learning about Needfinding, and collaborating with my groupmates for paper prototyping. I learned that Needfinding is telling about your objectives to people so you can uncover the user needs and opportunities for improvement .The most important thing that I learned about Needfinding is to have an empathy towards user’s way of interacting the interface of your application, not only you will be able to find the problem of your application’s interface, but it can also spur your team to have an idea and utilize that idea to implement the solution to the specific problem. The moment I attained this knowledge makes me realize how important to learn about Needfinding before becoming a UI/UX developer. Therefore, Needfinding is one of the fundamentals that shouldn’t be forgotten. Other facts that I learned about Needfinding:

  • Focusing on needs last longer than specific solution.
  • Efficient when it comes to problem solving, because the user itself will just tell you the problem of your interface.
  • Provides a roadmap for development.

When it comes to groupwork, collaboration between your group members is essential to good paper prototyping. A group needs to understand one another to make a good project. Without collaboration your project will only result in failure. As a member of a group, no one should be left out when it comes to brainstorming for a project, otherwise, you’ll be left out of the topic, as simple as that. Therefore, I conclude that, Needfinding is about knowing the user needs and come up with a solution to it. Collaboration is essential to a groupwork, so communicate, communicate, and communicate to your group members, because it matters.
