Reflection #5





I genuinely enjoyed SoCIT Fest, especially the seminars that I attended to. I learned different kinds of technology like IoT, Data Analytics, Cloud computing: Introduction to Microsoft Azure, etc. I also
participated in the event organized by an organization named JISSA: I forgot the name of the event, I think it’s called Hacking: Capture the Flag, something like that, but you get the point of the event, right? Anyways, I personally think this is the most fun event that I’ve participated in the SoCIT Fest, because It was one of my childhood dreams to do some hacking, and yes, I’m a hacker wannabe.
I’m one of the organizers in an organization called JPCS and I only took part as a manpower in one event, which is Fame or Shame.
Likewise, the event that I enjoyed the most is an event handled by JISSA, which I forgot the name of the event. The only thing that’s lacking on that event is the demonstration on how the hacking problems is solved. After the event, the organizer only shows the answer of the problems without displaying on how the problems are solved. My expectation for the next year’s SoCIT Fest is to be more organized than the previous SoCIT Fest.
